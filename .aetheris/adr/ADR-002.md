# ADR-002: Engineering Knowledge Base Is The Durable Source Of Engineering Truth

Status: Accepted
Owner: Principal Systems Engineer
Approval Date: 2026-07-01

## Context
Downstream planning and execution need durable evidence.

## Problem
Recomputing or inferring knowledge on every step causes inconsistency and drift.

## Decision
Use EKB objects and graph records as the durable knowledge plane.

## Alternatives
- Keep data only in transient memory
- Store untyped markdown summaries

## Trade-Offs
- Requires checksum/version discipline
- Enables impact analysis and traceability

## Consequences
- All durable knowledge must be typed and queryable
- Corrupt records are rejected
